What To Expect

R Church is a diverse church located in Fruitland, MD on 305 East Main Street 399. Our family consists of people from different cultures and backgrounds, yet we are united in our love for God and our joy in sharing His love with others. This church provides a place of freedom that eliminates barriers.

Our Mission Statement

We are a fellowship that recognizes the need for repentance (constantly turning from self to GOD) and rest (full reliance on Jesus) to produce peace and healing to the soul, spirit and body. Through repentance and rest we grow in the love of GOD, are drawn closer to GOD, and have a desire to share his revelations with each other and our community through relationships.

Common Questions

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?
Wear your shorts and flip-flops, or your Sunday's best; we’re not a church that worries about how you look on the outside. We meet you where you are, whether you’ve been attending church your whole life or you’re brand new to this whole thing.
WILL THERE BE A NURSERY?
Grown-up church services aren’t fun when you’re a kid, so we have a special place just for them! Your children are important to us, that’s why R Church creates a safe and fun place for your kids to hang out with other kids while learning to live like Jesus, too.
DID I JUST READ THAT SERVICES ARE ON SATURDAY NIGHTS?
Yes It is true! Our Worship services are on Saturday nights. That way you can have your whole day Saturday and all of Sunday to rest before starting your work week!
